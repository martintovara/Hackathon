The first VRBĚTICE Hackathon, a unique 24-hour challenge that brings together experts, students, and enthusiasts from both technical and humanities fields. This event marks the 10th anniversary of the attacks in Vrbětice and invites participants to tackle real-world issues in cyber security, OSINT, AI, and strategic communications.

Work in interdisciplinary teams, solve critical challenges, and present your solutions to a panel of experts. The best projects may become part of real defense strategies, helping protect critical infrastructure and national security.

Be part of Czechia's response to modern threats.

  • For students, professionals, and enthusiasts in tech, cybersecurity, AI, and more.
  • 24-hour hackathon solving real-world security challenges.
  • Open to diverse disciplines – no coding experience required.
